About Us

Welcome to Grandulo's Steakhouse. Located in Uptown Montgomery, Grandulo's offers elegance and sophistication while preserving the traditional charm of a historic steakhouse. Grandulo's most prized accolade is the reputation for making each patron's special occasion extraordinary. Our staff ensure privacy and upmost discretion while providing impeccable service. The meals, created by master chef Phillip Von Cleve, are exquisite and his special desserts provide the perfect finishing touch for the ultimate dining experience.
